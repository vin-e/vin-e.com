# Welcome to my digital garden

This is finally, well hopefully, going to be a home for me to start writing. I have been a developer longer than I am willing to admit on this first post and I have always struggled at writing because I felt that the epic multi-thousand word blog posts were not in my wheel house. The [digital garden](https://joelhooks.com/digital-garden) format is definitely a more comfortable space for me to write.

The term seemed familiar to me but the first time that it clicked for me was when I heard it from [David Poindexter](https://www.dpoindexter.com), aka [RobertTables](https://twitch.tv/roberttables), a streamer on twitch. Then immediately soon after, or maybe it was around the same time, it reaffirmed when I saw [Chris Bascardi](https://www.christopherbiscardi.com/) host a round, er square, table about digital gardens (and other topics). Writing quick posts, whether complete or not, in the public was the thing for me.

In future posts I expect to write about my current tasks and technical issues I find along the way. I will first write about my integrations of my eleventy blog with tailwind css. Then I might touch on some recent explorations like react native and gradle builds.

I appreciate [Joel Hooks](https://joelhooks.com/digital-garden), Chris Bascardi, and lil Bobby Tables for their inspiration to get started. And let me also shout-out the [Party Corgi](https://www.partycorgi.com/) discord community. Although I only recently joined and I am a fairly quiet member, the conversations, encouragement and progress that each and every one of you is doing is truly truly inspiring. Thanks!
